DEFUSE | Tactical Neural Override
Biological Reset for High-Anxiety & Panic States
DEFUSE is a high-performance Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to manually override the "Neural Hijack" that occurs during panic attacks. Unlike traditional meditation apps that ask you to "just breathe," DEFUSE uses Bilateral Stimulation (EMDR), 10Hz Alpha Binaural Audio, and Tactile Physics to force the nervous system into a stabilized state.
🛠 Features
• Neural Hijack Engine: 20+ randomized cognitive tasks that force brain activity to shift from the Amygdala (emotion) to the Prefrontal Cortex (logic).
• Visual Reset (EMDR): A silk-particle physics engine that facilitates bilateral eye movement through finger-tracing.
• Tactile Breath Ring: A synchronized 4-8 breathing halo that follows the user's touch to trigger the Vagus nerve.
• Binaural Audio: Real-time 10Hz Alpha-wave generation via WebAudioAPI to encourage relaxed alertness.
• Tactical UI: High-contrast, low-stress dark mode with instant "Emergency Exit" and haptic feedback.

🧬 The Science
DEFUSE operates on three physiological pillars:
1. Bilateral Stimulation: Moving eyes horizontally while focusing on a task reduces the vividness and emotional power of distressing thoughts.
2. Binaural Beats: By playing slightly different frequencies in each ear (e.g., 200Hz and 210Hz), the brain perceives a "beat" at the difference (10Hz), which is the frequency of relaxed, non-aroused brain states.
3. Vagal Toning: The 4-8 breathing cycle (Exhale twice as long as Inhale) manually stimulates the Vagus nerve to lower heart rate and blood pressure.
